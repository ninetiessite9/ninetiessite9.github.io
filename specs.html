Use Case Analysis

Why would someone pick us over Facebook?

  • Creative Control: We allow users to choose their own design guidelines
  • Internet Island: Nowadays whenever you join a new social media site you're instantly prompted to add all of your friends from various other sites. We allow you to reset your online presence and be who you want to be.
  • Curated Content: Our target audience is people born from 1990 - 1999, so that is the age of most of our users. Older people who are likely to fill your feed with content that doesn't interest you won't exist on our site because they won't be here. They won't get it and that will keep us "cool" while other social media sites drop in popularity from the younger generation.
